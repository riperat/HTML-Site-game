Mage

Mages are practitioners of magic, the ability to attain objectives or acquire knowledge or wisdom using supernatural means. They have strong attacks that use mana but they have low hp and def.

Assasin

The assasin is the person you pray that you dont see in your life. They have med hp, def, and attack.

Warrior

Warriors are special people that have trained since youth to fight against everything and anything they come across. They have high hp and def but low attack.
